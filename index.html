Nairobi is the capital and largest city of Kenya, located in the south-central part of the country. It is bordered by Kiambu County to the north and west, Machakos County to the east, and Kajiado County to the south. The city is a regional hub for transportation, education, information technology, research, and commerce, and it is a major tourist destination known for its nearby wildlife reserves and vibrant culture. Nairobi is also home to many international organizations and serves as Kenya’s political, economic, and administrative center.
