Utah

Salt Lake Temple is a sacred and iconic landmark of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, located in the heart of Salt Lake City, Utah. It is surrounded by Temple Square and the bustling downtown area, with the Wasatch Mountains rising to the east and the Great Salt Lake lying to the northwest. The temple serves as a spiritual center for worship, family ordinances, and religious education, and it draws millions of visitors each year for tours and special events. Renowned for its striking granite architecture and six soaring spires, the Salt Lake Temple stands as a symbol of faith, dedication, and the rich pioneer heritage of the Latter-day Saints.
